About Us

SHIVSHANKAR BIOCOAL ENERGY is a modern biomass briquettes manufacturing unit dedicated to converting agricultural waste into clean, affordable, and sustainable fuel solutions for industries. We support India's green energy mission while helping farmers manage agri-waste efficiently.

Located in the MIDC Area of Jintur, Pungala, Maharashtra, our facility is equipped with state-of-the-art machinery to produce high-quality biomass briquettes that serve as a reliable alternative to traditional fossil fuels.

What We Do

Biomass Briquettes Manufacturing

We produce premium biomass briquettes from agricultural waste, ensuring consistent quality and high calorific value for industrial use.

Agri-Waste Collection & Management

Comprehensive collection and processing of agricultural waste, turning potential environmental hazards into valuable resources.

Industrial Fuel Supply Solutions

Reliable bulk supply of biofuel solutions tailored to meet the energy needs of various industries across Maharashtra and beyond.
